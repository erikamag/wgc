Whole genome comparison of maize assemblies

November 02, 2018

Pipeline components: - minimap2 was used to align one assembly to another; - blat chain/net tools were used to process alignment resuls and build synteny chains/nets; - bcftools and GATK4 were used to call variants; - snpEff was used to evaluate variant effects on sytenic genes
